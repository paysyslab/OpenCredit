OpenCredit – Platform & Solution Overview​

Welcome to OpenCredit, a powerful and scalable digital lending platform designed for banks, fintechs, and financial institutions. OpenCredit enables organizations to launch, manage, and optimize lending operations end-to-end — from loan origination and credit assessment to disbursement, repayment, and collections.

This document provides a complete overview of the OpenCredit platform, including its solution architecture, core modules, and transaction flows across multiple channels and integrations.

OpenCredit Architecture​

OpenCredit delivers a comprehensive modular architecture to support the full digital lending lifecycle. It includes a loan origination engine, loan management system (LMS), middleware switch for integrations, and API gateway for seamless connectivity with internal and external systems.

The architecture is designed to ensure scalability, flexibility, and real-time processing, enabling institutions to handle high-volume lending operations efficiently.

Vision & Introduction​

We aim to empower financial institutions with innovative lending solutions that drive financial inclusion, operational efficiency, and intelligent credit decisioning.

OpenCredit provides a complete omni-channel lending ecosystem supporting:

Digital loan onboarding & origination Credit assessment & scoring Loan disbursement & lifecycle management Repayment scheduling & tracking Collections & recovery management Compliance & risk controls Product, fee, and interest configuration Real-time reporting & analytics

The platform is built for speed, intelligence, and interoperability.

1. Platform Overview​

OpenCredit enables institutions to manage:

  • Consumer & business loans
  • Loan products with configurable terms
  • Interest rates, fees, penalties, and taxes
  • Repayment schedules (EMI, bullet, flexible)
  • Customer lifecycle from onboarding to closure
  • Operational, financial, and regulatory reporting

2. Solution Overview​

OpenCredit is an end-to-end lending solution designed to handle the complete lifecycle of loans — from application to closure.

It allows institutions to define and manage loan products for individuals and businesses while ensuring seamless onboarding, automated decision-making, and efficient repayment handling.

The platform incorporates:

  • Loan Origination System (LOS)
  • Loan Management System (LMS)
  • Rule-based credit decision engine
  • Real-time transaction processing
  • Integrated onboarding & KYC verification
  • API Gateway for integrations
  • Middleware switch for third-party connectivity

2.1 Key Features​

  • End-to-end digital lending lifecycle
  • Configurable loan products and policies
  • Automated credit scoring & decisioning
  • Real-time disbursement and repayment tracking
  • Flexible repayment and rescheduling options
  • Built-in compliance and risk controls
  • API-first architecture for integrations

2.2 Core Modules​

🔹 Loan Origination System (LOS)​

Manages the initial stages of lending:

  • Digital application submission
  • Customer onboarding & KYC
  • Credit scoring & eligibility checks
  • Approval workflows

🔹 Loan Management System (LMS)​

Handles post-disbursement lifecycle:

  • Loan account creation
  • Repayment scheduling (EMIs, installments)
  • Interest and penalty calculations
  • Loan restructuring & rescheduling
  • Early settlements and closures

🔹 Product Setup​

Enables configuration of loan products:

  • Loan types & categories
  • Interest rates & calculation methods
  • Tenure and repayment structures
  • Fee and penalty rules
  • Eligibility criteria

🔹 Master Setup​

Supports foundational configurations:

  • Customer segments
  • Channels
  • Currencies & regions
  • Fee structures
  • Risk parameters

🔹 Credit Decision Engine​

Automates lending decisions:

  • Rule-based scoring
  • Risk profiling
  • Policy enforcement
  • Integration with external credit bureaus

🔹 API Gateway​

Facilitates secure integrations:

  • Loan applications & onboarding
  • Disbursement & repayment APIs
  • Account statements
  • Third-party integrations

🔹 Recurring Jobs (CBA)​

Supports automated background processes:

  • EMI generation & scheduling
  • End-of-day processing
  • Interest accruals
  • Late payment penalties
  • Reporting & reconciliation

🔹 User Interface​

Provides operational and customer touchpoints:

  • Digital loan application portal
  • Back-office management
  • Dashboards & analytics
  • Reports & monitoring tools

🔹 Loan Account Management​

  1. Loan Disbursement Handles approval-based disbursement with validations, compliance checks, and fund transfers.

  2. Loan Closure Supports closure scenarios including full repayment, early settlement, or write-offs.

🔹 Collections & Recovery​

Manages overdue loans:

  • Automated reminders & notifications
  • Penalty application
  • Recovery workflows
  • Legal escalation support

🔹 Integration with Allied Platforms​

Supported via OpenConnect:

  • Credit bureaus
  • AML/CFT systems
  • NADRA VeriSys / BVS
  • Payment gateways (RAAST, 1LINK)
  • SMS / Email services
  • ore banking systems

Supports REST, SOAP, ISO8583, ISO20022 protocols with standardized communication.
